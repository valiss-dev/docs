# NNNN. Short title of the decision

- Status: proposed | accepted | rejected | superseded by [NNNN](NNNN-title.md)
- Date: YYYY-MM-DD
- Deciders: <names>

## Context

What problem is being decided? Forces at play, constraints, requirements. State
the situation as it is, without the answer.

## Decision

The decision, in the active voice: "We will ...". Be specific and unambiguous.

## Consequences

What becomes easier or harder as a result. Positive, negative, and neutral
outcomes. Follow-up work this creates.

## Alternatives considered

Options that were weighed and why they were not chosen.
